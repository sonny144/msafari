Our Story

Founded in Windhoek, Mufasa Safaris and Tours CC is a local travel agency with over 15 years of experience crafting exceptional travel experiences in Namibia, Botswana, and Tanzania. The founder, began as a passionate local guide and has since built a company dedicated to showcasing the beauty and cultural richness of Southern Africa to both French and English-speaking travelers.

Our Mission: Sustainable Tourism

Namibia, a pioneer in environmental protection, inspires our commitment to responsible tourism. We strive to minimize our impact on the environment, support local economies, and preserve cultural heritage, ensuring that our journeys contribute positively to the destinations we explore.
